Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Polarix AI (“we”, “our”, or “the Service”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information across all our products, including Luma Mind and My Story House, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information across our products:

  • Account information (via Firebase Authentication or other login systems)
  • App usage data (features used, session counts, interactions)
  • User-generated content (storybooks, drawings, meditation entries)
  • AI-generated requests (text or voice input for meditation guidance or learning)
  • Device and technical information (device type, OS version)

My Story House – Children’s Learning App

  • Voice input for book creation and stroke animation (processed temporarily for conversion to text, not stored long-term)
  • AI-assisted topic suggestions and coloring (enhances child’s own drawings, no external content generated)
  • Stroke order data sourced from open-source repositories
  • User-created storybooks can be exported as PDFs; users control sharing

Luma Mind – Meditation App

  • AI-generated meditation text and audio via Google Gemini and Microsoft Azure TTS
  • Tracking of meditation sessions and usage patterns to improve app experience
  • User input may be processed by AI for personalized content; data is not used for AI model training

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core app functionality for each product
  • Improve performance, features, and user experience
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse
  • Provide customer support

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising. Some products may use third-party services (Google Firebase, Google Gemini AI, Microsoft Azure TTS, open-source stroke data), which may process data temporarily according to their own privacy policies.

4. User-Generated Content

Content created by users (storybooks, drawings, meditation entries) belongs to the user. Users control sharing (exporting PDFs or using app features). We do not distribute user content without consent.

5. Security & Data Storage

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Data is stored securely in cloud services compliant with industry standards.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations.

7. Children’s Privacy

For products aimed at children (My Story House), we comply with COPPA, GDPR-K, and Apple Kids category requirements. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Parental Controls

My Story House includes features that are safe for children and do not require account creation for most functionality. Parents are encouraged to supervise app use.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted within the app and on our website with the new effective date.

10. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and stored data
  • Request corrections to your information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or any of our products, contact us at:
